TOP 3 MOVIES ACCORDING TO JHONNY

My top 3 movies of all time

In no particular order

  • The Godfather

    • The Godfather. This movie doesn't need an explaination. Study of Godfather's script and story writing is a part of any budding director's, story writer's or script writer's curriculum.

  • Grave of the fireflies

    • Grave of the fireflies is a animated materpiece set in world war 2, and Japan. The movie revolves around a pair of siblings and what the war makes them go through. Be ready for tears and loneliness after during the movie. Viewer discretion advised. Watch with your partner for comfort.

  • The secret life of Walter Mitty

    • Movie starts with an average guy trying to get attention of a women on a dating website. He describes how boring and average his life has always been. But as his life progresses, takes twists and turns, and his job is threatned, Walter goes on an life altering journey and takes the audience with him. As the movie ends Walter's life changes along with the audience. This movie stays with the viewer and have log lasting impact.
